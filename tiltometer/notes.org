*** Connections:
**** VDD   - Arduino 3.3v
**** GND   - Arduino GND
**** INT   - Arduino digital pin 2
**** FSYNC - leave unconnected
**** SCL   - Arduino SCL (dedicated pin or Analog 5)
**** SDA   - Arduino SDA (dedicated pin or Analog 4)
**** VIO   - Arduino 3.3v
**** CLK   - leave unconnected
**** ASCL  - leave unconnected
