* GPS unit conversions:
** Example of raw data we receive from the device:
*** 3816.1754N, 08541.9944W
*** N = +, S = -
*** E = +, W = -
** This converts into the following DMM value:
*** Lat: 38.161754, Long: -085.419907
** Which in turn converts into:
*** +38° 9' 42.3144", -85° 25' 11.6646" lat, long
